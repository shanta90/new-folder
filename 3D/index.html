The Golden Fiber

Derived from the fertile deltas of Bengal, our artisanal jute fibers provide the organic essence that makes Bengal Brew truly unique. Sustainable, earthy, and refined.

Himalayan Essence

Pure Himalayan tea leaves, hand-picked at peak freshness. We blend the deep, oxidized notes of premium tea with our jute-infused craft soda for an unparalleled tasting experience.

Spiced Ginger Root

Sourced from the mineral-rich soils of the delta, our raw ginger root adds a warm, invigorating kick. It is carefully cold-pressed to release intense, aromatic oils without compromising the gentle nature of our brew.

The Path to the Pour

Selection

Only the top 2% of Bengal jute fibers are chosen for their purity and aroma.

Extraction

Precise cold-pressure extraction captures the life-force of the jute plant.

Fusion

Himalayan tea and jute essences are married in small-batch ceramic vats.

Maturation

A 48-hour resting period allows the complex flavor profiles to harmonize.

Carbonation

Fine-bubble carbonation is infused at freezing temperatures for maximum crispness.
